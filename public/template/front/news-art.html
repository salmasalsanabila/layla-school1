Student Art Exhibition

Published on 30 January 2026

Creative artworks by students were displayed for the community, including:

  • Paintings and drawings
  • Photography exhibitions
  • Sculptures and installations
  • Mixed media projects

The exhibition aimed to nurture artistic talent, confidence, and expression among students, and was open to parents, teachers, and visitors. Selected artworks will also be part of an online gallery to showcase the students' creativity.
